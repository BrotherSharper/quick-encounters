  2. You didn't have an open Journal Entry with Actor links in it

How to use Quick Encounters

Keep this Journal Entry open and follow along!

Method 1

  1. Drag your Hostile tokens to the Scene map (either by moving existing tokens, or dragging Actors) 
  2. Select the tokens you want to use in the Quick Encounter
    • If you want, change their visibility by right-clicking and tapping the Toggle Visibility option
  3. Press the  Quick Encounters "fist" button (in the left control bar).
  4. You'll see a new Quick Encounter Journal Entry open with Actor links representing your tokens.
    • If you want, add descriptive text to read when you run this Encounter
    • You might also pick a folder for this Journal Entry (especially if you have a folder per Scene)
    • Notice that your tokens have disappeared off the map! Don't worry, their positions have been saved in the Journal Entry
  5. Close the Journal Entry and you will see a Quick Encounter Map Note dialog. Change the title, font-size, and icon if you want.
  6. Press the Save Map Note button to automatically place a single map pin on the Scene map close to where the tokens were
    • (If you accidentally Close the Map Note it won't be saved, but you can just drag the Quick Encounter Journal Entry to the map to re-create the Map Note)
  7. To run your Quick Encounter, simply double-click the map pin to open the Quick Encounter Journal Entry
  8. Then click the embedded Add to Combat Tracker button; all your saved tokens will be positioned on the map and added to the Combat Tracker, ready to fight!
    • You can also click the Quick Encounter "fist" button instead of the embedded button

Method 1 duplicates the "Encounter" functionality available in Fantasy Grounds.

 

Method 2

  1. Create a Journal Entry and describe your Encounter or open an existing Journal Entry (like the ones you'd find in a purchased module).
  2. Drag Actors into your Journal Entry (you can put a number in front of the Actor link to get multiple of them)
  3. Close the Journal Entry
  4. Go to the Journals list, find the Quick Encounter Journal Entry and drag it to the Scene map at the encounter position.
  5. To run your Quick Encounter, simply double-click the map pin to open the Quick Encounter Journal Entry.
  6. Then click the Quick Encounter button: your saved tokens will be positioned around the map note, and added to the Combat Tracker, ready to fight!
    • In this method, your tokens are always Invisible when added to the map

 

What Now?

You can just close this Journal Entry as is and it will be deleted automagically!

Or you can delete all this text and turn it into your first Quick Encounter Journal Entry by following Method 2 above. Good luck!
